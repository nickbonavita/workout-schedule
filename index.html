General Workout Advice

For lifting, you typically want to do 3 or 4 sets with a weight that you will begin to fail or lose good form on between the 8th and 12th rep.

All exercises should be slow and controlled, not speedy.

If your form starts to become bad later in a rep, you can consider this failing.

The three-day cycle should be push, pull, legs.

Core and cardio can either be incorporated into those days or into their own days, but all days should have at least some light cardio.

Heart rate training is usually broken into zones from easier effort to near-max effort.

Roughly 80% of your activity should be in Zone 2 (easy to moderate, steady pace).

The remaining 20% should aim for Zone 4/5 (hard to very hard efforts).
